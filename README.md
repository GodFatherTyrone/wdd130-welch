# wdd130-welch
Making Logans website
Front Porch Coin Co.

Purpose:
The purpose of Front Porch Coin Co. is to provide free, expert in-person coin appraisals directly at the customer’s home. The site explains the service, builds trust, and allows users to easily schedule an appraisal. 

Audience:
Individuals in the Rexburg area who own coins—collectors, families with inherited coins, hobbyists, and anyone wanting trustworthy, no-hassle, face-to-face appraisal services.
